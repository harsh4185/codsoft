Top Brand
Patek Philippe

A deluxe watch is a wristwatch that is made for a specific person or event. The watch can be made for the general public or given as a present.

Rolex

Despite the soaring popularity of Rolex women’s and men’s watches, each timepiece is still made by hand. Internationally recognized as a status symbol, Rolex watches are forever in high demand.

Jaeger‑LeCoultre

Jaeger-LeCoultre is the top watch brand of the Richemont group, with a storied history going back to 1833. This inventive watch manufacturer is responsible for over 1,200 calibers.
